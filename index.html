Hi! I'm Sidharth Jaishankar.

I am a type and graphic designer from Chennai, India. I design and code typefaces, primarily for Tamil and Latin.

I worked as a type designer at Ek Type, Mumbai, and attended EsadType, a post-graduate course in typeface design at Ésad Amiens. Projects I have worked on include Katha Tamil, Kodai Tamil & Latin, YouTube Sans Tamil, Honk, and Naatak Tamil.

As a global docent at the Letterform Archive, I help curate, write, and present tours through their online archive on subjects like type history, digital typography, artists’ books, and Indic type.

I occasionally post lettering on Instagram. For any inquiries or to just say hi, please send me an email.
